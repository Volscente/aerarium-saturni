# Reading List

Bibliographies and links to source material. A structured list of books that form the intellectual foundation of the Codex. Organized by theme, with notes on key arguments and relevance.

## Foundations

| Book | Author | Key Argument |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *The Four Pillars of Investing* | William Bernstein | Theory, history, psychology, and business of investing |
| *A Random Walk Down Wall Street* | Burton Malkiel | Markets are efficient; index funds beat active management |
| *The Intelligent Investor* | Benjamin Graham | Value investing principles and margin of safety |

## Behavioral Finance

| Book | Author | Key Argument |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *Thinking, Fast and Slow* | Daniel Kahneman | Cognitive biases that distort financial decision-making |
| *Your Money and Your Brain* | Jason Zweig | Neuroscience of investor behavior |

## Retirement & FIRE

| Book | Author | Key Argument |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| *Die with Zero* | Bill Perkins | Optimizing life experience vs. over-saving |
| *Work Optional* | Tanja Hester | Practical FIRE planning framework |
