# Mathematics

Financial math formulas and quantitative tools. This page catalogs the essential mathematical machinery used in finance, from time-value-of-money identities to option pricing models like Black-Scholes.

## Topics

- Time value of money: PV, FV, and discounting
- Compounding: continuous, annual, and periodic
- Expected return and variance of a portfolio
- The Black-Scholes formula and its derivation
- Duration and convexity for fixed-income securities
- The Sharpe, Sortino, and Calmar ratios
