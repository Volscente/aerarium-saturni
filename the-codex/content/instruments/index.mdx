# Instruments

A taxonomy of financial securities. This section classifies the main asset classes available to investors, explaining their structure, risk profile, and role within a portfolio.

## Contents

- **Equities** - Stocks, ownership rights, and dividend basics.
- **Fixed Income** - Bonds, treasury bills, and interest rate sensitivity.
- **Pooled Funds** - ETFs, Mutual Funds, and Index Funds.
- **Commodities** - Gold, raw materials, and the case for real assets.
- **Bitcoin & Crypto** - Bitcoin's mechanics, security model, and role as a financial instrument.
