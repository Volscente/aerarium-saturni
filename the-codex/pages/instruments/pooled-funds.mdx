# Pooled Funds

ETFs, Mutual Funds, and Index Funds. Pooled investment vehicles allow investors to gain diversified exposure to a basket of securities through a single instrument. This page compares the main structures and examines their cost, tax, and liquidity implications.

## Topics

- Mutual funds: active management and NAV pricing
- Index funds: passive replication and tracking error
- ETFs: exchange-traded mechanics and creation/redemption
- Total Expense Ratio (TER) and its compounding drag
- Distributing vs. accumulating share classes
- Domicile, UCITS compliance, and withholding tax considerations
