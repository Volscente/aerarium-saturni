# FIRE Movement

Safe Withdrawal Rates (SWR) and FIRE levels. Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE) is a framework for accumulating enough capital to live indefinitely off investment returns. This page explains the math behind withdrawal rates and the spectrum of FIRE variants.

## Topics

- The 4% rule and the Trinity Study
- Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR): definition and Monte Carlo validation
- Sequence-of-returns risk and its impact on early retirees
- FIRE variants: Lean FIRE, Fat FIRE, Barista FIRE, Coast FIRE
- The FI number formula: annual expenses ÷ SWR
- Accumulation phase: savings rate as the key lever
