# Net Worth

Tracking assets, liabilities, and computation methods. Net worth is the fundamental measure of personal financial health. This page defines how to inventory assets and liabilities accurately and track changes over time.

## Topics

- Definition: net worth = total assets − total liabilities
- Liquid vs. illiquid asset classification
- Valuing real estate, vehicles, and private investments
- Tracking frequency and tooling (spreadsheets, apps)
- The difference between net worth and investable assets
- Bernstein's 4-pillar principles as a framework for personal wealth
