# Brokers

Comparison of brokers, custodians, and safety. The choice of broker determines the cost structure, asset safety, and available instruments for an investor. This page evaluates the key dimensions for selecting and trusting a custodian.

## Topics

- Broker vs. custodian: roles and regulatory distinctions
- Segregation of client assets and SIPC/investor compensation schemes
- European brokers: DEGIRO, Interactive Brokers, Fineco, Directa
- Commission structures: per-trade, subscription, and spread-based
- Currency risk from broker's base currency
- Portability: transferring assets between custodians (in-kind transfer)
