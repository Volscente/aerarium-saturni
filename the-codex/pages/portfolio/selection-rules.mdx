# Selection Rules

Criteria for choosing specific securities. Once an allocation model is chosen, individual instruments must be selected to implement it. This page defines the quantitative and qualitative filters used to evaluate and rank candidates.

## Topics

- Tracking error and index replication quality
- TER threshold and cost efficiency criteria
- Fund size (AUM) and liquidity requirements
- Domicile and tax-treaty implications (Ireland-domiciled UCITS)
- Distributing vs. accumulating share class selection
- Rebalancing triggers and frequency rules
