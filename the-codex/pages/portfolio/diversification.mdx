# Diversification

Risk management, correlation, and the "Free Lunch." Diversification is the only strategy in finance that reduces risk without sacrificing expected return. This page explains why uncorrelated assets combine to produce a more efficient portfolio.

## Topics

- Systematic vs. unsystematic risk
- Correlation coefficient and the covariance matrix
- The efficient frontier and Modern Portfolio Theory (MPT)
- Why diversification is called the "only free lunch" in finance
- Over-diversification and its diminishing returns
- Geographic and asset-class diversification
