# Portfolio

The philosophy of asset allocation. A portfolio is more than a collection of assets — it is a structured system designed to meet long-term financial goals under uncertainty. This section covers the principles, models, and rules used to build resilient portfolios.

- **Models** — Famous portfolios (All-Weather, 60/40, Swensen).
- **Selection Rules** — Criteria for choosing specific securities.
