# Costs & Fees

Managing TER, MER, and hidden transaction costs. Investment costs compound against the investor in the same way returns compound in their favor. This page catalogs every layer of cost and quantifies the long-term impact of each.

## Topics

- Total Expense Ratio (TER) and Management Expense Ratio (MER)
- Transaction costs: bid-ask spread and brokerage commissions
- Currency conversion fees and FX spread
- Fund swing pricing and dilution levies
- The long-term compounding drag of a 0.5% TER difference
- Strategies for minimizing total cost of ownership
