# Taxation

Capital gains, dividends, and local tax efficiency. Tax drag is one of the largest and most controllable costs for an investor. This page covers how different types of investment income are taxed and strategies for minimizing the burden legally.

## Topics

- Capital gains tax: realized vs. unrealized, short- vs. long-term
- Dividend taxation and withholding tax (WHT) at source
- The Italian "regime dichiarativo" vs. "regime amministrato"
- Tax-loss harvesting: mechanics and wash-sale rules
- Double-taxation treaties and UCITS ETF domicile optimization
- Tax-advantaged accounts: PIR (Italy), ISA (UK), 401k (US)
