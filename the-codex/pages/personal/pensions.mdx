# Pensions

Pension schemas vs. private PAC strategies. State pension systems provide a baseline retirement income, but their adequacy varies widely by country. This page compares public pension architectures with private accumulation plans (PAC) and individual retirement accounts.

## Topics

- Defined benefit (DB) vs. defined contribution (DC) plans
- The three-pillar pension model (state, occupational, private)
- Italian pension system: INPS and the contributivo method
- Private PAC (Piano di Accumulo del Capitale): mechanics and tax treatment
- Individual pension funds vs. insurance-linked pension products
- Integrating pension income into a broader retirement income plan
